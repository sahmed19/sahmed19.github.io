#### Shadertoy Raycaster

*Written in GLSL*

Done as part of a Blizzard graphics test. I implemented:
* Point & directional lighting with PBR reflectance
* ambient lighting
* emissive lighting
* environment-mapped reflections
* directional shadows
* normal mapping
* bloom

View the ShaderToy here: [link](https://www.shadertoy.com/view/cd2GWW)
